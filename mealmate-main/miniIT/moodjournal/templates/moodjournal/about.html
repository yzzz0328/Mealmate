About Mood Journal

Welcome to the Mood Journal! This section of our MealMate app is dedicated for users to record entries of their thoughts and feelings throughout their day.

What is this for?

Trying to be consistent and managing one’s diet becomes harder when the user is unable to monitor their progress and moods they are feeling throughout their diet journey. This section would help with that by providing a public posting area for everyone to share their thoughts on. This could encourage people to stay motivated and keep on going!

How to Use?

Create an Entry

  1. Go to the “Create Entry” page via the first link on the sidebar labeled “New Entry”.
  2. Fill in the blanks. Guidance for the mood ratings are available at the bottom.
  3. Once everything is filled up, click on “Post” and it will redirect you to the home age with your new entry as the latest on top of the list.

View an Entry

  1. Click on the date of an entry to view a detailed page of the individual entry.

Update an Entry

  1. On the detailed entry page, click on “Update” to edit the entry.
  2. You are able to change any of the existing details on the entry.
  3. Submit changes by clicking on “Update” and it will direct you back to the home page.

Delete an Entry

  1. On the detailed entry page, click on “Delete” to remove entry.
  2. Confirm by clicking on “Yes, delete”.
  3. You will be redirected to home with the deleted entry gone.

Search for Entries from an Author

  1. In the field above the list, you can search for a specific author’s entry.
  2. Click on “Search” and it will show only the searched author’s entries.
  3. Click on “Reset” and it will redirect you to the original list.

How is Mood Color calculated?

At the Entry Creation page, the 5 integer fields are used to calculate the Mood Color. They would be summed and calculated an average score between 0 to 10. The scores are defined as follows:

  • 0 <= score < 2: Red (Worst)
  • 2 <= score < 4: Orange (Bad)
  • 4 <= score < 6: Yellow (OK)
  • 6 <= score < 8: Green (Good)
  • 8 <= score <= 10: Blue (Super)
